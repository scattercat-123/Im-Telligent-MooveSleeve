Hey, Welcome to Im-Telligent - MoodSleeves

Moodsleeve is an all-in-one mood assistant. It allows you to gauge your mood, reflect on your feelings and consciousness, and navigate your daily life. Moodsleeve's founders, Karna Amborkar and Atharv Sharma, seek to make mental wellness literally at everyone's fingertips.

Karna Amborkar - The Software technologist

The founder of Moodsleeves, Karna, is a technologist and designer with a experience in software programming, and now he wants to help you understand your mental activity like never before. His ultimate dream is for you to never take your mind for granted ever again.

Atharv Sharma - The Tech Wizard for hardware

The tech whiz is Atharv, who makes sure everything runs smoothly. He possesses the and hardware skills to balance everything so that moodsleeves can detect your mood 24/7. He is the wizard behind all the hardware with his companion working on software. He merely cares that the product's end user does not experience any glitches.

What We Do

We're all about tech that boosts your ability to achieve mental wellness. Moodsleeves allow you to monitor your feelings with immediate feedback, all while maintaining awareness of longer-term trends. Think of it as a mental health app—only better, and more personalized.

Track Your Feels

moodsleeves helps you track your feelings every day so you can learn to interpret what all those thoughts swirling in your brain might mean. It's like a sweater for your moods.

Customize It

Moodsleeves are adjustable. They're tailored to your needs. You can change it in any shape or form, adjusting it according to how you feel..

Real-Time Feedback

moodsleeves is like having a therapist in your pocket with suggested mood-enhancing interventions just an arm's reach away! moodsleeves is your in-your-pocket pal for suggestions when you need them the most.
